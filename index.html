Explore the World!

I'm Cherry Shy Sabarias, a passionate traveler with a wanderlust that knows no bounds. This blog is my way of sharing my experiences, tips, and insights from exploring the world. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or a first-time traveler, I hope to inspire you with stunning photography, captivating stories, and practical advice. Join me as I delve into diverse cultures, uncover hidden gems, and savor the beauty of our planet.

Grab your passport, pack your bags, and let's embark on this journey together!

BORACAY

Escape to paradise on Boracay, where pristine white-sand beaches meet crystal-clear turquoise waters. Sunbathe, swim, or simply lose yourself in the beauty of this world-famous island. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or unforgettable nightlife, Boracay has something for everyone.

CAGSAWA RUINS

Step back in time at the haunting Cagsawa Ruins, a poignant reminder of the destructive power of nature. The crumbling church, partially buried by volcanic ash, offers a powerful glimpse into the Philippines' past. Capture breathtaking photos against this dramatic backdrop and experience a unique piece of history.

PUERTO PRINCESA

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the heart of Puerto Princesa, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Explore the awe-inspiring underground river, marvel at the diverse wildlife, and lose yourself in the breathtaking natural beauty of this hidden gem.

PICNIC GROVE TAGAYTAY

Escape the city bustle and enjoy panoramic views of Taal Volcano at Picnic Grove. Take a leisurely stroll, ride the cable car, or indulge in a delicious picnic amidst the cool mountain air. It's the perfect place to relax, reconnect with nature, and create lasting memories.

INTRAMUROS MANILA

Step into a world of Spanish colonial charm in Intramuros, Manila's historic walled city. Wander through cobblestone streets, explore ancient churches and forts, and immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of this fascinating destination.

BANAUE RICE TERRACES

Witness the awe-inspiring Banaue Rice Terraces, a testament to the ingenuity and artistry of the Ifugao people. These ancient rice paddies, carved into the mountainside, are a UNESCO World Heritage site and a true marvel of engineering. Experience the beauty of this unique landscape and learn about the rich cultural heritage of the Ifugao people.
